Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to approve a gate pass request?

Typically, requests are processed within 24 hours. Emergency requests are prioritized.

What reasons are typically approved?

Medical appointments, family emergencies, academic requirements, and official college events are usually approved.

How do I use my QR code at the gate?

Once your request is approved, show the QR code to security personnel at the gate. They will scan it to verify your exit permission.
